Help & Support

How to book a ticket

  1. Enter From, To, and Date, then click Search.
  2. Select a bus from the results.
  3. Fill passenger details and confirm booking.
  4. Check your bookings under the Bookings page.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel or reschedule my ticket?

Cancellation/reschedule will be added soon. For now, contact support with your booking ID.

I didn’t receive confirmation

Open the Bookings page to verify. If missing, try booking again or contact support.

Payment options

Online payments will be added in a future update. Currently bookings are recorded without payment.
